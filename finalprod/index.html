Tamales

Ingredients:

Ingredients for Dough
- 30 washed corn husks , 1 1/2 cups Chicken broth , 1 packet of Maseca for Tamales (corn masa) , 1 cup Pork lard , 12 pieces Tomato skins Salt to taste
Ingredients for Filling:
- 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts ,1 large white onion , 4 green tomatoes , 2 optional green chilies , 1 sprig of cilantro , 1 garlic clove , Salt to taste

Instructions:

Chicken Filling:
1. Cook tomatoes, chilies, onion, garlic, and chicken in water with a pinch of salt until chicken is cooked.
2. Blend tomatoes, chilies, onion, garlic, and cilantro (excluding water).
3. Shred chicken and mix with the blended sauce.
Dough:
1. Boil tomato skins with water.
2. Melt lard, add corn flour, baking powder, salt, chicken broth, and tomato water. Stir until smooth.

Assembly:

1. Soak corn husks in hot water to soften.
2. Place dough on husks, add chicken fillin.
3. Fold husks around filling to create tamales.
4. Steam tamales until cooked and dough is firm.
